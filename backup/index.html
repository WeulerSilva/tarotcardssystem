Backups exist to assist in preventing data loss, such as files accidentally deleted due to physical or human failure. This ensures the integrity of data, configurations, databases, and user files.

The Tarot Cards System It accomplishes this practically. Without backups, many data become simply irrecoverable if lost due to an accidental failure or a computer invasion, for example.

Backups are part of the operational routine of its systems and follow a determined policy. They are performed as automated as possible, reducing the impact on the work of administrators and system operators.
