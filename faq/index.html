FAQ (Common questions)

  • Home
  • FAQ (Common questions)

Can I have as many attendants as I wish?
A: You can, but we work with service blocks.
The first block entitles up to 10 simultaneous attendants.
From this point onwards, each block entitles you to 10 attendants and so on.
It works this way due to data processing between appointments.
To find out the prices, please get in touch using the contact form on the home page and one of our consultants will get in touch.

I already have a website, can I apply the Tarot System to my website?
A: Yes, in most cases we can reuse the layout.

How does System Backup work?
A: The servers to accommodate backups are located in 2 different countries, therefore, we increase our safety margin against catastrophe, for example.
We separate it into two processes:
The first will perform a disk image backup, performed daily in the early hours of the morning, meaning the site will not suffer a loss of performance during a possible service.
The second is responsible for the database and scripts.

Does the system have Anti Hacker technology?
A: Yes. The Tarot System has several commands that help protect data and access the backoffice panel.

Can I customize the system?
A: Yes, but it depends a little on availability, processing resources and delivery time according to our availability.

What payment methods is the system integrated with?
A: The system is delivered with a standard payment method, for example Paypal or PagSeguro.
If you wish to use other payment methods, they may be made available upon approval of the budget.

I want to create the website but I don't have a logo, can you create it?
A: Yes. We create the logo upon budget approval. We have an average delivery time of 10 working days.

Does the system have social media sharing?
A: Yes, it is possible to put a link to your Facebook Fanpage so that people can like and share the website.

How does website support work?
A: Support operates during business hours from 08:00 to 12:00, 13:30 to 18:00, from Monday to Friday.
Server support operates on an on-call basis. We provide a cell phone number on call within the Customer Service Center..

I want to set up a Tarot website to serve clients, how does it work?
A: The website works with minute control for your attendants and you define a value in % (percentage) that they will receive for serving the customer.
The attendants will have an available balance in the account and you, the manager, will be able to write off this balance when you agree on the account balance with the attendant.
