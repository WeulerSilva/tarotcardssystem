Custom Chat

  • Home

  • Custom Chat

Attending via custom chat guarantees quality, ease and agility in customer service.

Check out some advantages in using the chat:

1. Increases the company's direct communication channels

The relationship with the company starts to be done in real time.

2. Higher sales

Chat abandonment rates are lower than telephony

A confused customer is a lost chance to show your quality in the market. You can chat with the customer at the time of access.

3. Low cost for the company

Reduces phone costs. Personalized chat is cheaper than call plans.

4. Simultaneous calls

An operator can serve several customers at the same time.

5. Confidence

Your client will feel safer knowing that you have a trained team ready to serve you, transmitting trust and seriousness through your website.

6. Professional Communication

The use of a personalized chat to communicate with customers instead of popular communicators demonstrates seriousness and business professionalism, in addition to avoiding conversations that are not consistent with the company's main activity.
